Origin

 Chapter 0: The Eye That Looked Inward (Overview)

One man finally completes the journey of self-realisation, and becomes an immortal psychic God of Creation after he dies. With his new creative powers, he decides to reform the self-realisation process - making it a tad bit easier for others to gain access to such powers, but on a lower scale. This new process is based completely off of the ideas of magic, where people could become powerful Magicians.

At the same time, he creates a new process that is mainly accessible through 'scientific' means - despite personally viewing magic and science as one and the same. This process allows people to become Assistants instead, who are simply given helpful and passive powers. He does create at least one Magician whom he entrusts with the 'Assistant Formula'.

Simply put: Magicians are typically ugly, but more powerful; and Assistants are typically beautiful, but more passive.

After bringing every other person and creature into the world - merely out of his own imagination - he finally decides to create a lover for himself, before changing his own form, nullifying most of his powers and wiping his own memory. It was his utmost desire to live in his own world as a side character.

"Self realisation truly is the most powerful magic of them all."
