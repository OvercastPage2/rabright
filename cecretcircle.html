The Cecret Circle universe is ever expansive. There are no limits to one's imagination. All Magician and Assistant characters gain their power through making self-realisations. So if you were to connect the philosophy of Cecret Circle to your own life, then who would you become? What would be your story? And just how powerful do you think you'd be?

"The path self-realised is always the right one." - Robert Adam Bright

Introduction

 Chapter 0: The Eye That Looked Inward (Overview)

One man finally completes the journey of self-realisation, and becomes an immortal psychic God of Creation after he dies. With his new creative powers, he decides to reform the self-realisation process - making it a tad bit easier for others to gain access to such powers, but on a lower scale. This new process is based completely off of the ideas of magic, where people could become transformed based on their own self-realisations.

Simply put: Magicians are typically ugly, but more powerful; and Assistants are typically beautiful, but more passive.

After bringing every other person and creature into the world - merely out of his own imagination - he finally decides to create a lover for himself, before changing his own form, nullifying most of his powers and wiping his own memory. It was his utmost desire to live in his own world as a side character.

"Self realisation truly is the most powerful magic of them all."
